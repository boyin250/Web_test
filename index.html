Product

我们团队正在研发新一代“解码–编码一体化”的双向闭环植入式脑机接口系统，突破传统脑机接口只能单向读取脑信号的限制，实现人脑与外部设备之间的真实双向交互：既可高精度解码运动意图，也能通过神经电刺激向大脑回传触觉、压力等反馈，使瘫痪患者能够像使用触屏一样自然地与机器互动。系统以高密度软电极阵列、解码–刺激协同算法、微创可逆植入与实时闭环架构为核心，已完成动物实验验证并与多家顶尖临床机构合作，正推进工程样机走向临床验证，目标成为中国首个具备量产能力的高通道双向闭环植入式脑机接口平台。

Our team is developing a next-generation, fully integrated bidirectional closed-loop implantable brain–computer interface (BCI) that overcomes the one-way limitation of traditional BCIs that only “read” neural signals. The system not only decodes motor intention with high precision, but also delivers neural electrical stimulation to return tactile and pressure-like feedback to the brain—enabling paralyzed patients to interact with devices as naturally as using a touchscreen. Built on a high-density soft electrode array, coordinated decode–stim algorithms, a minimally invasive and reversible implantation approach, and real-time closed-loop feedback control, the platform has been validated in animal studies and is now advancing an engineering prototype toward clinical validation with leading hospitals. The project aims to become China’s first scalable, mass-producible high-channel bidirectional closed-loop implantable BCI platform for restoring interaction capabilities in patients with severe paralysis.

Team

团队由许泽宇博士带头（牛津大学临床神经博士、华山医院神经外科医生），核心成员具备临床神经科学、生物医学工程、电子电气工程、航空工程等交叉学科的博士/博士后训练与工程化经验，覆盖高密度软电极、神经信号解码与刺激算法、植入式系统与微创可逆植入方案、闭环控制与安全验证等关键环节。团队已与国内多家顶尖临床机构建立合作，正推进工程样机走向临床验证，目标打造具备量产能力的高通道双向闭环脑机接口平台。

The team is led by Dr. Zeyu Xu (DPhil in Clinical Neurosciences, University of Oxford; neurosurgeon at Huashan Hospital) and brings interdisciplinary PhD/postdoctoral training and engineering experience across clinical neuroscience, biomedical engineering, electrical/electronic engineering, and aerospace engineering. This end-to-end capability covers high-density soft electrodes, neural decoding and stimulation algorithms, implantable system engineering and minimally invasive reversible implantation, closed-loop control, and safety validation. In collaboration with leading clinical centers in China, the team is advancing an engineering prototype toward clinical validation, aiming to build a scalable, mass-producible high-channel bidirectional closed-loop BCI platform.
