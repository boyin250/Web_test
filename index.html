我们的使命
Our Mission

让失去行动能力
的人重获自主

Restoring autonomy
to those who lost it

全球超过 2700 万人因脊髓损伤、ALS 或其他神经系统疾病失去自主运动与表达能力。Neuronic 相信，脑机接口技术能够成为这些患者与世界重新连接的桥梁。

Over 27 million people worldwide have lost the ability to move or communicate due to spinal cord injury, ALS, or other neurological conditions. Neuronic believes BCI technology can become the bridge reconnecting these patients to the world.



我们的第一代产品 N1 专为运动皮层信号采集设计，帮助患者通过意念操控假肢、轮椅和电脑。

Our first-generation device, N1, is designed for motor cortex signal acquisition — enabling patients to control prosthetics, wheelchairs, and computers through thought alone.

01

超高密度柔性电极阵列

Ultra-high-density flexible electrode array

自主研发的聚合物柔性探针，宽度仅为人发的十分之一。1024 通道同步采集，植入创伤极小，长期生物相容性经 18 个月灵长类实验验证。

Proprietary polymer flexible probes — one-tenth the width of a human hair. 1024-channel simultaneous recording with minimal implant trauma, validated over 18 months in primate studies.

02

端侧神经解码 ASIC 芯片

On-device neural decoding ASIC

专为神经信号设计的定制芯片，在设备本地完成实时解码，延迟低至 0.1ms，无需传输至云端，从设计层面保护患者数据隐私。

A custom chip designed specifically for neural signals. Real-time decoding at 0.1ms latency, entirely on-device. Patient data never leaves the device — privacy by design.

03

无线双向通信协议

Wireless bidirectional communication

突破性低功耗无线协议，支持高带宽神经数据双向传输，同步实现神经刺激反馈回路，完全无经皮导线，降低感染风险。

Breakthrough low-power wireless protocol enabling high-bandwidth bidirectional neural data and stimulation feedback, with no transcutaneous wires — minimizing infection risk.
